Where to find great urban art!?

Urban (Art)chive aims to archive public art from all around the city; both preserving and making it easily accesible. Because these installations are sometimes ephemeral (particularily the street art), not all of these artworks will still be here.

But why not walk around your city and see which artworks you can find accross the urban landscape? Don't forget to send us your latest discoveries so we can add it to the archive.

Share your experience with us and your friends on twitter and instagram with the hashtag #urbanartchive!
